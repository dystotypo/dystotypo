Dystotypo

 Your dystopic magazine to an utopic world

Here you are going to find a typographic journey throughout time and dystopian stories. Decide which story you want to follow among the following options: 1984, Fahrenheit 451 or Brave New World. Your choice might seem without consequences to you, but depending on what you choose, you'll be thrown in a world of its own totalitarian kind...

About this project

Disclaimer

The purpose of this web site is to explore various types of typographic and layout style for text documents, as an end-of-course project for the "Information Modelling and Web Technologies" course of the Master Degree in Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge of the University of Bologna, held by professor Fabio Vitali.

The documents contained in this web site have been selected for their length and complexity from various websites and magazines. Their publication here is not intended to be an alternative or replace their original locations.

1984

Big Brother is watching you!

WAR IS PEACE FREEDOM IS SLAVERY IGNORANCE IS STRENGTH

This is the slogan of the totalitarian government in which our main character, Winston, lives under control. Any sort of movements are monitored 24/7 by the telescreens and the Thought Police is the organization making sure that no one will elaborate any plans or discussion for a different world.

Stability was practically assured
