Dystotypo

 Your dystopic magazine to an utopic world

Here you're going to find a font journey thorughout time aruond dystopian stories. Decide which story you want to follow among the following options: 1984, Fahrenheit 451 or Brave New World. Your choice might seem different to you, but at the end you'll be stuck in a world in which you think everything works but it's full of issues underneath.

About this project

Disclaimer

The purpose of this web site is to explore various types of typographic and layout style for text documents, as an end-of-course project for the "Information Modelling and Web Technologies" course of the Master Degree in Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge of the University of Bologna, held by professor Fabio Vitali.

The documents contained in this web site have been selected for their length and complexity from various websites and magazines. Their publication here is not intended to be an alternative or replace their original locations.
