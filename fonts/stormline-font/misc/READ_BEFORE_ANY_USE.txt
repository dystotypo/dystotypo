Stormline is a sleek inlined typeface, drawn and created by Mans Greback in 2021. 

Its wide, carved out letterforms works with decorative minimalism, and retains the purity of a clean sans-serif typeface while being modern and original. 

Stormline is provided in three distinct weights: Medium, Bold and Black. 

The font is built with advanced OpenType functionality and has a guaranteed top-notch quality, containing stylistic and contextual alternates, ligatures and more features; all to give you full control and customizability. 
It has extensive lingual support, covering all Latin-based languages, from Nordic to South Africa, from America to South-East Asia. 
It contains all characters and symbols you'll ever need, including all punctuation and numbers.
